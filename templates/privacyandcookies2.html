SCOPE ERP System Privacy and Cookies Statement:

At SCOPE ERP System, we value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy and Cookies Statement explains how SCOPE ERP System processes your personal data and the use of cookies when you interact with our ERP system.

Personal Data Collection:

SCOPE ERP System collects personal data from you when you interact with our ERP system. This data may include information you provide directly to us, as well as data collected from your interactions, usage, and experiences with our ERP system. The data we collect depends on your choices, privacy settings, and the features you use within SCOPE ERP System. We may also obtain data from trusted third parties for specific purposes.

How Personal Data is Used:

We use the collected personal data to provide you with an efficient and effective ERP system experience. The purposes for which we process your data include but are not limited to:

  • Providing and improving our SCOPE ERP System, including updates, troubleshooting, and support.
  • Personalizing your experience within SCOPE ERP System and making relevant recommendations.
  • Understanding user behavior to improve and develop our ERP system further.
  • Conducting targeted marketing and advertising based on your preferences and interests.
  • Operating our business efficiently, meeting legal obligations, and conducting research.

Sharing Personal Data:

At SCOPE ERP System, we respect your privacy, and we do not share your personal data with third parties without your consent, except in specific situations where sharing is required to fulfill transactions or provide requested services. We may share data with trusted partners and vendors who assist us in delivering the ERP system and its functionalities. Additionally, we may share your data in response to legal obligations or to protect the safety and rights of SCOPE ERP System and our users.

Access and Control of Personal Data:

We believe in empowering you with control over your personal data. SCOPE ERP System provides various tools and settings that allow you to access and manage your personal data. You can exercise your data protection rights by contacting us or using the tools provided. However, certain data access and control may be limited as required by applicable law.

Cookies and Similar Technologies:

SCOPE ERP System uses cookies and similar technologies to enhance your user experience, store preferences, and analyze site usage. Cookies enable us to provide personalized services and tailored content to better serve you. By using SCOPE ERP System, you consent to the use of cookies as described in our Cookies Policy.

Enterprise and Developer Products:

If you are using SCOPE ERP System as part of an organization or as a developer, additional privacy information may apply. Please refer to the relevant sections in our Privacy Statement for Enterprise and Developer Products.

Collection of Data from Children:

SCOPE ERP System is intended for adult users. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children without parental consent. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal data, please contact us, and we will take appropriate action.

Your Privacy Choices:

At SCOPE ERP System, we are committed to transparency and user control. We encourage you to review our privacy settings and product-specific details to tailor your ERP system experience according to your preferences. For more detailed information on how we protect your privacy, please refer to the SCOPE ERP System Privacy page.

SCOPE ERP System strives to maintain the highest standards of privacy protection and is dedicated to continuously improving our data handling practices to safeguard your personal data.
